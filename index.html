Why Iconic Productions?

Iconic Productions’ Founder, Luz R. Valdez has the ability to bring people with diverse backgrounds to collaborate in an effective manner. Her multicultural interests and desire to showcase diverse media has led to an array of multicultural productions. Together we are a team inspired by the challenge to break down cultural and linguistic barriers to deliver effective customized audiovisual products and services. A high client satisfaction rating occurs when we are successful in carrying out the company mission. We understand that every client business has growth potential and we deliver flexibility around the clock to work with every type of schedule to meet client needs. We work towards exceeding client expectations and take pride in producing a client's final product vision.

Iconic Productions LLC provides tailored creative and innovative products and services for audiovisual projects and communication campaigns. We are a team of passionate artists and audiovisual professionals with multilingual and multicultural backgrounds looking for constant creative and innovative solutions for your audiovisual projects and communication campaigns. Our diversity allows us to deliver creative and innovative solutions.
