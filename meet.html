Luz R. Valdez, Executive Producer, Director

Luz graduated from the Russian Film School (VGIK) with a focus on Directing Documentary Films and TV.
She launched Iconic Productions in 2010 and contributes more than 20 years of production industry leadership, audiovisual expertise and executive management experience to her current role. Past freelance roles include Producer, Director, Assistant Director and Editor. In addition, Luz has experience as an Audiovisual Instructor for two prominent Universities in Peru and as the Assistant Director for theater performance plays done by Escena Latina.

Irina Slotsova,
Technical Director

Irina has an extensive technical experience in working for major news network in shooting and editing news
in linear and non-linear equipment. She has also directed TV shows in studio and produced commercials, educational videos
and live performances. Irina holds a BS in Telecommunications from Volga Telecommunications University, Samara, Russia.

Rosita Maeks-Anderson,
Project Manager

Rosita has an MBA from Babson College and a BA in Psychology from UMass Dartmouth.
Her involvement in project development spans across various industries including a Women’s Leadership Satellite in retail banking,
a Peruvian child sponsorship program for a non-profit, and marketing and digital design for local startups in the Boston area.

Andrey Kneller, Adaptation Specialist/Translator

Andrey Kneller was born and grew up in Moscow, Russia. His work has appeared in a number of literary magazines and journals,
including National Forum, Gentle Reader, Unlikely Stories, and the Hypertexts. He has also published several books of poetry translations,
including the works of Vladimir Mayakovsky, Boris Pasternak, Anna Akhmatova, Marina Tsvetaeva, and Alexander Pushkin.
